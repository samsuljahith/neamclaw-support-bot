# TechNova — Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## 30-Day Return Window

All items purchased from TechNova may be returned within **30 calendar days** of the delivery date. The delivery date is confirmed by the carrier's tracking information.

## Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return, items must meet ALL of the following conditions:

1. **Original packaging:** The item must be in its original, undamaged packaging.
2. **Unused condition:** The item must not show signs of use, wear, or damage.
3. **All accessories included:** All included accessories, manuals, and cables must be returned.
4. **Proof of purchase:** You must provide your order ID or receipt.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items **cannot** be returned:
- Opened software or digital products
- Gift cards
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
- Custom or personalized products
- Items damaged by the customer (not covered under warranty)

## Restocking Fee

- Items returned in original sealed packaging: **No restocking fee**
- Items returned opened but in like-new condition: **15% restocking fee**
- Items returned with missing accessories: **25% restocking fee** (or return denied)

## Refund Process

1. Contact support with your order ID to initiate a return.
2. We will email you a **prepaid return shipping label** (US only; international customers pay return shipping).
3. Ship the item back within **7 days** of receiving the label.
4. Once received and inspected (1–2 business days), your refund will be processed.
5. Refunds appear on your original payment method within **5–7 business days**.

## Exchanges

We offer direct exchanges for the same item (different color/size) or a different item of equal or lesser value. For items of greater value, you will be charged the difference.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item:
- Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery with photos of the damage.
- We will send a replacement immediately at no charge.
- No need to return the damaged item in most cases.

## Holiday Extended Returns

Items purchased between November 15 and December 31 may be returned until **January 31** of the following year.
