# TechNova — Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?
You can track your order by providing your order ID (e.g., ORD-12345) or the email address you used during checkout. Our support bot can look up your order status instantly.

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard Shipping:** 5–7 business days (free on orders over $50)
- **Express Shipping:** 2–3 business days ($9.99)
- **Overnight Shipping:** Next business day ($19.99)

All shipping times are from the date of shipment, not the date of order.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. International shipping takes 10–15 business days and costs vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

### My order hasn't arrived yet. What should I do?
If your order is past the expected delivery date:
1. Check your tracking number for the latest status.
2. Wait an additional 2 business days (carriers sometimes experience delays).
3. If still not received, contact us and we'll investigate or issue a replacement.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in original, unopened packaging. See our detailed Return Policy document for full terms.

### How do I start a return?
Contact our support team with your order ID. We'll provide a prepaid return label. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

### Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Yes! Contact support with your order ID and the item you'd like to exchange for. Exchanges are subject to availability.

### When will I get my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund goes back to your original payment method.

## Products

### Are your products covered by warranty?
Yes. All TechNova products come with a 1-year limited warranty. Extended warranties (2-year and 3-year) are available for purchase. See our Warranty document for details.

### A product I want is out of stock. When will it be available?
We restock popular items every 2–4 weeks. You can sign up for back-in-stock notifications on the product page, or ask our support bot to check availability.

### Do you offer price matching?
We offer price matching within 14 days of purchase if you find the same product at a lower price from an authorized retailer. Contact support with proof of the lower price.

## Account & Payment

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

### How do I create an account?
Visit technova.com/register and follow the prompts. You can also check out as a guest without creating an account.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your registered email.

## Contact

### How can I reach a human agent?
If our support bot can't resolve your issue, you can request escalation to a human agent at any time. Human agents are available Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM EST.

### What are your support hours?
Our AI support bot (Nova) is available 24/7. Human agents are available Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM EST.
