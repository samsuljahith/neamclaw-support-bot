# TechNova — Shipping Information

## Domestic Shipping (United States)

| Method             | Delivery Time        | Cost                          |
|--------------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|
| Standard Shipping  | 5–7 business days    | Free on orders over $50; $4.99 otherwise |
| Express Shipping   | 2–3 business days    | $9.99                         |
| Overnight Shipping | Next business day    | $19.99                        |

- Orders placed before **2 PM EST** on business days ship the same day.
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends ship the next business day.
- Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

- Log into your TechNova account to view order status.

## Delivery Issues

### Package shows "delivered" but I haven't received it:
1. Check with neighbors or building management.
2. Look for a delivery notice or check your mailbox.
3. Wait 24 hours — packages sometimes scan early.
4. Contact us if still missing after 24 hours.

### Package is damaged on arrival:
- Take photos of the damaged packaging and item.
- Contact us within 48 hours.
- We'll send a replacement at no charge.
