# TechNova — Warranty Information

## Standard Warranty

All products sold by TechNova include a **1-Year Limited Warranty** from the date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## What's Covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Component failures under normal use
- Dead pixels on screens (if more than 5 dead pixels)
- Battery defects (capacity drops below 80% within warranty period)
- Connectivity issues (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth) caused by hardware defects

## What's NOT Covered

- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Water or liquid damage (unless the product is rated waterproof)
- Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, fading)
- Software issues or third-party accessory damage
- Damage from power surges (use a surge protector!)

## Extended Warranty Plans

| Plan              | Duration      | Cost                    |
|-------------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| TechNova Shield   | 2 years total | 10% of product price    |
| TechNova Shield+  | 3 years total | 18% of product price    |

Extended warranty plans must be purchased within 30 days of the original product purchase. They extend the standard warranty and also cover:
- Accidental damage (1 incident per year)
- Battery replacement
- Free expedited shipping for warranty repairs

## How to Make a Warranty Claim

1. Contact TechNova support with your order ID and a description of the issue.
2. Our team will troubleshoot to determine if the issue is covered.
3. If covered, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label for you to send the item.
4. We will repair or replace the item within 10 business days.
5. If repair is not possible, we'll issue a full refund or replacement.

## Warranty Repair vs. Replacement

- We attempt repair first for items over $100.
- Items under $100 are typically replaced outright.
- If your exact product is out of stock, we'll offer an equivalent or superior model.

## Proof of Purchase

Please keep your order confirmation email or receipt. Your order ID is sufficient for warranty claims — we maintain records for all purchases.
